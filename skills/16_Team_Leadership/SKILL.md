# 16 — Team & Leadership (L5)

**Level:** L5 — CTO & Business.

## Goal

Org structure, hiring, and culture are architecture decisions too — Conway's Law (systems mirror the communication structure of the organizations that build them) means team topology and software architecture are two views of the same underlying problem, and getting one wrong undermines the other.

## Why This Belongs in a Technical Decision Engine

A Principal Engineer or CTO who only reasons about technology and ignores team structure, hiring reality, and culture will design systems the organization can't actually build, staff, or maintain — this domain exists to keep technical recommendations grounded in organizational reality, the same way `skills/02_Product/` keeps them grounded in business reality.

## Reference Files

- `reference/team-topology-org-design.md` — team structures (stream-aligned, platform, enabling, complicated-subsystem teams) and how they should track architecture boundaries.
- `reference/hiring-engineering-talent.md` — hiring for actual stage-appropriate needs, role definition, and avoiding common hiring-architecture mismatches.
- `reference/technical-leadership-culture.md` — engineering culture, technical leadership style, and the behaviors that compound into a healthy or unhealthy engineering organization.
