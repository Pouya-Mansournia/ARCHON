# 17 — Cost & Business Architecture (L5)

**Level:** L5 — CTO & Business.

## Goal

Cost is a first-class architectural constraint, not an afterthought reviewed only when a cloud bill causes alarm — Cost Awareness is one of the 8 Core Principles (`skills/00_Core/reference/core-principles.md`) and sits explicitly in the Engineering Decision Principles priority order, above Scalability and Performance. The goal here is to make cost trade-offs visible and deliberate at decision time, not discovered after the fact.
